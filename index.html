About Me

I am a student of computer science, apprentice game developer, musician, and obvious nerd. I have always had an affinity for electronics, whether it was making my first web page at about six years old (albeit with help) or my recent endeavors to make video game. Perhaps more obsessive than my love for computers is that for games. Last on the list of obsessions is my enjoyment for playing the piano, clarinet, and tenor saxophone.

Growing up, I loved to play all kinds of games. I've been making games for as long as I can remember. The first ones were simple, pencil and notebook required. Over the years they grew in scale, grandeur, and fairness. The first game I made (that I consider now as a success) was a board game for a sixth grade project.
